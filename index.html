Welcome to The Mood Lab..we've been expecting you. We are happy you decided to drop in and chat with us. At The Mood Lab we believe in putting your best self forward in order to achieve a healthy, happy, full life. The steps we help you take to achieve this is through our Flotarium Pods. These pods were designed keepiing your mind and body in mind! We put clean air into the pod so there will be no stale or stuffy air. Our pods are 9ft long, almost 6 feet wide and over 4 feet tall making them one of the largest pods in the industry.

Why float? Floating offers a second healing therapy, sensory deprivation. Sensory deprivation is the removal of stimuli from one or more of the senses, this allows you to mentally relax in a way that is similar to sleep. To achieve this level of sensory deprivation in our float tanks we have soundproofed each room, we have made the temperature of the water the exact temperature of your body to remove the sense of touch, and we have removed any light source from interrupting your float. In spite of the goal of sensory deprivation, each tank is provided with a relaxing light that can be turned on or off for the convince of the floater.
